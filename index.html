About Project

Redefining Virtual Communication

Built for Modern Businesses

Local Seminar Meeting is an advanced web-based platform engineered specifically for local companies seeking professional, reliable, and feature-rich online meeting solutions.

Our platform combines cutting-edge WebRTC technology with intuitive user experience design, delivering HD video quality, real-time collaboration tools, and enterprise-grade security. Whether hosting team meetings, client presentations, or company-wide seminars, our solution ensures flawless communication.

Developed by a dedicated team of students from Hanoi University of Science and Technology, this project represents our commitment to solving real-world business challenges through innovative technology and exceptional engineering.
